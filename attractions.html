Top Attractions in Rawalpindi

Discover the most popular tourist spots, parks, markets, and historical landmarks in Rawalpindi. Explore the rich history and vibrant culture of this fascinating city.

Ayub National Park

Ayub National Park is a popular spot for nature lovers. It offers serene landscapes, a zoo, and various recreational activities for visitors of all ages.

Rawalpindi Murree Road

Rawalpindi Murree Road is a scenic route that connects Rawalpindi to the hill station of Murree, offering picturesque views and several stops for sightseeing.

Faisal Mosque

The Faisal Mosque is an iconic architectural masterpiece in Islamabad, located on the Margalla Hills, offering stunning views and a peaceful atmosphere.
